With the current situation, our future is definitely uncertain. The hunt for jellyfish has turned from an attempt to curb over population, to a profit driven business. Will the generations that come after us still be able to know of such an animal? Or would we drive the jellyfishes to extinction, only to repeat the whole process with yet another marine life?
